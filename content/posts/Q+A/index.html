Q&A: Learn more about Rika!

Feel free to contact me and ask any questions!

Gender and Sexuality

Q: When did you come to know that you are transgender?

A: I have been experiencing gender dysphoria since childhood, but I didn't know the word "transgender" until 2018. At first, I found it difficult to understand its meaning because people used it in different ways and was not sure if it described me. However, after learning about gender issues for years, now I'm quite sure that I am transgender.

Language Learning

Q: Which languages do you recommend in 2022?

A: If you have no idea, I recommend you choose from French and German. However, you should master English first.

Others

Q: What are your favorite film and novel?

A: My favorite film is possibly Patema Inverted, a 2013 Japanese animated science fiction film written and directed by Yasuhiro Yoshiura. I almost haven't read any novels recent years, but I would like to consider Yasunari Kawabata's Snow Country as one of my favorite. I read it when I was in high school and I still remember the last sentence even now. As you see, my aesthetic sense is fundamentally East Asian, even though I hate East Asia so much.
