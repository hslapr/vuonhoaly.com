Feel free to contact me and ask any questions!

Gender and Sexuality

Q: When did you come to know that you are transgender?

A: I have been experiencing gender dysphoria since childhood, but I didn't know the word "transgender" until 2018. At first, I found it difficult to understand its meaning because people used it in different ways and was not sure if it described me. However, after learning about gender issues for years, now I'm quite sure that I am transgender.
